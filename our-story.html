Our Story

Born from a love of French tradition and a desire to bring something truly special to the neighbourhood — we are a bakery-café where every pastry tells a story and every visit feels like a warm welcome.

2018 — The Beginning

It started in a small home kitchen in Paris, experimenting with croissant doughs late into the night.

2021 — Opening Day

We opened our first café with just six items on the menu and a queue out the door from day one.

Today — Still Baking

Every morning we still start from scratch, the same way we always have — just with a few more familiar faces.
